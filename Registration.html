Notes:

  • Student discount is only available if the 1st author of the paper is a student. Valid proof required.
  • Each registration covers 6 pages. Additional pages are charged.
  • Extra paper discount: second paper gets 10% off, third paper gets 20% off.
  • Listeners are welcome and can register online or onsite.
  • City tour requires separate payment and reservation.
  • If you need to apply for invitation letters, email by October 10.

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancellations must be made in writing.
  • Before July 1, 2025: 70% refund.
  • After July 1, 2025: No refund.
